DEFUSING IMPROMPTU VIOLENCE
Select the Next Button to continue.

There is no one correct way to resolve a potentially violent incident.  How you respond very much
depends on the aggressor’s behavior and the overall situation.  Methods include:

  • Exercising restraint and being aware of your own feelings and emotions so that you don't escalate the situation
  • Communicating in a respectful manner
  • Being flexible and cooperative
  • Calling for assistance
  • Reporting the situation to the local WVP Coordinator or your Supervisor
