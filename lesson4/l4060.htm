REPORTING REQUIREMENTS
Select the Next Button to continue.

If you witness inappropriate behavior, be prepared to report the following facts to the local Workplace Violence Prevention Coordinator:

  • Who:
    • Who was the aggressor
    • Who or what was targeted
    • Who else was present
  • What:
    • What was said, using exact quotes as much as possible
    • Physical actions that accompanied the inappropriate behavior
  • When:
    • Date and time of the incident
  • Where:
    • Place where the incident occurred
  • Whether the aggressor has acted inappropriately before

Click the worksheet on the right to print a list of these questions that can guide you when making a report.   The worksheet can also be found under “Resources.”
