PROTECTING YOURSELF FROM INTENDED VIOLENCE
Select the Next Button to continue.

If you are faced with an emergency or a situation in which violence is about to occur, do the following:

  • If possible, remove yourself from the situation.
  • Take cover in a secure location.
  • If possible, call out for help or dial 911.
  • Once you are safe, report the situation to your Supervisor or to the WVP Coordinator.
